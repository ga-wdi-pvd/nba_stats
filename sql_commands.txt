All columns for all players from the New York Knicks (NYK)
Answer: SELECT * FROM players WHERE team = 'NYK';
All columns for all players from the Indiana Packers (IND) who are under 26 years old
Answer: SELECT * FROM players WHERE team = 'IND' AND age < 26;
All columns for all players, ordered from least points scored to most points scored
Answer: SELECT * FROM players ORDER BY points;
Name and Points per game (points/games), for the players with the top 20 points per game
Answer: SELECT (points/games) AS points_per_game FROM players ORDER BY points DESC LIMIT 20;
The average age for all players
Answer: SELECT AVG(age) AS average_age FROM players;
The average age for all players on the Oklahoma City Thunder (OKC)
Answer: SELECT AVG(age) AS average_age FROM players WHERE team = 'OKC';
The average age for all players who played more than 40 games
Answer: SELECT AVG(age) AS average_age FROM players WHERE games > 40;
The team and total points scored from all players on that team (team points), ordered from most team points to least
Answer: team, SUM(points) AS team_points FROM players GROUP BY team ORDER BY team_points DESC;
BONUS QUESTIONS:
Age and the average points per game for that age, ordered from youngest to oldest
Answer: SELECT age, AVG(points/games) AS avg_points_per_game FROM players GROUP BY age ORDER BY age;
Team and the the number of players who score above 12 points per game on that team, ordered from most to least
Answer: SELECT team, COUNT(*) AS players FROM players WHERE (points/games) > 12 GROUP BY team ORDER BY players DESC;
